Holofit: Indoor Fitness Experience

时间   2018.10-2018.12

四人项目

职责   用研流程规划设计/用研结果分析/功能导出/界面设计


Research

In order to gain insights about target users' expectations of fitness product, we interviewed 12 people who have experience in using indoor fitness products. Based on the interview results, we drew conclusions on existing features they found most neccessary, as well as expected features that are temparorily missing in existing products.


The existing products mainly collect data during the exercising, and then presenting the feedback after the exercise. However, under this mode, the user cannot receive timely feedback and adjust their pose accordingly.

Goals

Feedback that is more timely and intuitive.

Instructions that is more detailed and personalized.

Natrual and uninterrupted ways of interacting.
