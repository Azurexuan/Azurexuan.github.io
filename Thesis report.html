Enabling Human-In-The-Loop Interpretability Methods of Machine Learning Models: The Case of Bird Species Identification

Time 2021.04-2021.11

Overview

In my graduation project, I explored ways to put in use machine learning interpretability models for general citizens with the conceptualization of a citizen science platform, to help the data scientists enhance their models while allowing the end users to learn bird knowledge in an intriguing way. Along the journey, I worked closely with a data scientist (inventor of an ML interpretability model), bird experts, and birding enthusiasts, to understand their needs and expectations and carried them on into the final design strategy.
